Replit tutorial

For our lessons we suggest that you use Replit for your coding. If there are any other text editors or IDEs that you want to use it works fine as well. Here is a tutorial for how to use Replit:

  1. 1. Go to replit.com
  2. 2. Sign up with your gmail or your email
  3. 3. Press "Create Repl" and click on "Python" under templates
  4. 4. Name your "Repl" however you want to
  5. 5. Click "Create Repl" and wait for the Repl to load
  6. 6. Start writing your code here and press "Run" when you are ready to test it.
  7. 9. See if the code works. If it doesn't, there'll be errors that show up here.
  8. 10. To exit the Repl, click the icon on the top right hand corner and click profile. Then on the left side menu click on "Home"
